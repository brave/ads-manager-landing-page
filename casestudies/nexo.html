Nexo's Challenge

Nexo wanted an advertising platform that could meet their business needs while ensuring their customers’ personal information was secure. Traditional advertising sends a lot of personal information to advertisers, ad exchanges, and other entities in order to target ads. This information can be sensitive and given Nexo’s privacy-savvy user base and community, they needed an advertising platform that offered privacy-respecting ads to an audience that had opted-in to view them.


Solution

Nexo chose Brave User Ads because of its capability to advertise to a very specific audience which immediately resulted in a 20% cost-saving in customer acquisition cost over traditional advertising channels. When setting up their campaign, Nexo had the opportunity to assign content categories (200+ IAB-defined categories) to their campaign for interest and contextual alignment. They did not want to overwhelm users with unnecessary content, but carefully curated high impact messaging that captured the attention of hundreds of thousands of Brave users.

Nexo also ran a “Campaign with a Cause” using Brave’s Sponsored Image to give millions of Brave users the opportunity to donate their Basic Attention Token (BAT), to Save the Children. The #SaveWithCrypto campaign, in partnership with The Giving Block and the Nexo community, raised $25,000 USD to help feed hungry children. The Sponsored Image, along with a co-branded social media campaign gave Nexo and Save the Children the visibility they needed to make an impact on thousands of children.

The Brave Sponsored Image campaign resulted in more visibility for Nexo and raised funds for a good cause. That’s how we like to do marketing in the crypto community.

Lyubcho Kostadinov
Head of Marketing

Who is Nexo

Nexo is the world’s leading regulated financial institution for digital assets. The company’s mission from day 1 has been to maximize the value of digital assets by offering tax-efficient ‘Instant Crypto Credit Lines’, high-yield ‘Earn Interest’ products and ‘Send & Pay’ Capabilities for their clients, while ensuring the $100 million custodial insurance and military-grade security of the Nexo Wallet. Since raising $52.5 million in 2018, Nexo has processed $1.5+ billion in 40+ fiat currencies for 700,000+ users across 200+ jurisdictions.
