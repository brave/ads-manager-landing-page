Intel's Challenge

The Intel Gamer Days promotion needed to reach a niche group of gamers who were privacy and security savvy and, based on insights, were often disconnected from the digital advertising ecosystem due to ad blockers. Being the world's first global ad platform built on privacy, Brave User Ads were a clear choice when it came to reaching this audience in the most respectful and opt-in manner. Intel's main goal was to create buzz and excitement on social media to gain the attention of gamers worldwide and to amplify their messaging around various promotions.


Solution

Intel leveraged Brave User Ads on mobile and desktop in order to direct users to Twitter where over 1,000 users joined in on the conversation and promoted Intel Gamer Days by tweeting for a chance to win high value swag from Brave and Intel. This social accelerator magnified the impact of Brave User Ads and gave Intel Gamer Days the brand lift it needed to exceed their targets. Intel monitored a unique hashtag which helped to close the reporting and attribution loop to truly see the success of this campaign.

Intel reached 12.9% CTR with Brave User Ads

“Our innovative partnership with Brave, leveraging both Brave’s social driver format to create awareness and FOMO (fear of missing out) of the Intel Gamer Days promotions, as well as Brave User Ads to alert gamers of the OEM promotion opportunities, provided the highest CTR out of all platforms at 12.9%. The sweepstakes, with an opportunity to tweet for a chance to win Intel swag led to 1,000 users sharing an awareness tweet about the promotions.”

Amy Siegel, Director of Investment, Dentsu Aegis Network — Team Intel

What is Intel Gamer Days

Intel Gamer Days is Intel’s PC “gaming holiday” and buying frenzy lasting 16 days with curated deals, prizes and experiences. IGD is an integrated campaign inclusive of paid media, influencers, social media, events, streams and partner promotions. They set out to develop an unconventional campaign that drove FOMO (fear of missing out) with the elusive, ad-blocker prone PC gaming audience. They strived to produce a 100% banner free campaign, knowing this audience is opposed to traditional forms of advertising, and utilized innovative formats and technology to find the white space and break-through with this audience in a unique way.
